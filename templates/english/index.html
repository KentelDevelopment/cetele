Documentation and support



Registiration for students:


  1. Click the "Start now" button under the student title.
  2. Set e-mail and password.
  3. You can login to your account after your signing up ,you should click "Login" button.


Registiration for Teachers:

  1. Click the "Start now" button under the teacher title.
  2. Set your e-mail,password and full name to sign up.
  3. Click "Sign Up" button.
  4. You can login to your account after your signing up ,you should click "Login" button.



(Student) How can I add my question statics?


  1. After your logging in branches appers on the screen.
  2. Click on the branch in which you want to upload the number of questions and scroll down the page.
  3. Fill the form at the bottom of the page and confirm.


(Student) What is my username ?


  1. There is a wheel icon at the user interface that appears after you log in.
  2. After clicking this button you can see your information.


(Teacher) How can I add my students to my page ?


  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click the white "Add student" button which appears on your page.
  3. Enter the student username and school number to the form which was appeared.
  4. We will add the student after you click the "Add Student" button.


(Teacher) How can I send the "Zoom" address to my students ?


  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click to white "Add Online Class" button.
  3. Fill the form at the new tab and confirm it.
  4. We will send the zoom url to all of the students at your page.


(Teacher) How can I assign homeworks?


  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click the "Paper Plane" icon which was appeard on top right.
  3. Enter the homework title and description before clicking the "SEND" button.
